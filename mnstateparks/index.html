About

This promotional map highlights Minnesota’s state parks, recreation areas, and waysides with official “Park highlights,” hours, and links. Imagery appears where a Wikimedia/Wikipedia photo is available. Colors reflect Minnesota’s new state flag (light blue, dark blue, yellow, white). Designed by Web Mapper GPT.

Data: MN DNR statutory boundaries (polygons), park listings & details from the MN DNR parks A–Z site. Photos: Wikipedia/Wikimedia (licenses vary).
